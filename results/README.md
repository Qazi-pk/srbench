# SRBench Results

Results associated with the paper [La Cava, W., Orzechowski, P., Burlacu, B., de França, F. O., Virgolin, M., Jin, Y., Kommenda, M., & Moore, J. H. (2021). *Contemporary Symbolic Regression Methods and Their Relative Performance.*](https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.14351) are available in the `v2.0` folder.

Results for the paper [Imai Aldeia, G. S., Zhang, H., Bomarito, G., Cranmer, M., Fonseca, A., Burlacu, B., La Cava, W., & de França, F. (2025). *Call for Action: Towards the Next Generation of Symbolic Regression Benchmarks.* *Proceedings of the Genetic and Evolutionary Computation Conference Companion.*](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.03977) are organized into two folders:
- The `black-box-tuning` folder contains black-box results, where hyperparameter tuning was performed for each run.
- The `first-principles-tuning` folder contains the phenomenological and first-principles results, also obtained with hyperparameter tuning.
